Essential Westminster

We will visit the most important sights of Westminster - beautiful parks and royal palaces. Sights include- Buckingham Palace, Big Ben, the Houses of Parliament, Trafalgar Square and some other great places along the way. Take pictures with royal guards and red phone boxes. Hear about the lives and homes of our legendary Queen and royal family and some of London's wealthiest and most powerful people!

Morning tours- Often include the changing of the guard ceremony at Buckingham Palace (or top tips on the best places to see it from!)

Afternoon tours- Can include the Dismounting Ceremony at Horseguard's Parade (a great alternative to the changing of the guard.)
See here for changing of the guard times


City of London and Thames Path Walk

A walk through the City of London and beyond following the River Thames. Famous landmarks on this tour include St Paul's Cathedral, the Globe Theatre and London Bridge, with plenty of selfie spots overlooking the beautiful Tower Bridge.

We also pass by several sights with movie connections. Find out where Daniel Radcliffe went to school, visit the "Death-Eater" Millennium Bridge! As a local Londoner and Harry Potter fan, I can also share tips on other wizard-related to-dos.


East London Tour



Looking for something different? We discover a less explored side of London's history on this tour - disease, crime, poverty and murder. Hear about Jack the Ripper - the infamous East End serial killer. Also, learn about East London's diverse immigrant communities and take a closer look at some of Brick Lane's wonderful street art.

English Language Lessons

I have been teaching English as a foreign language to international students for many years. For English classes please see our family-run English school Facebook page or contact me for more information.

We offer classes online and in person to students of all English levels. Group and private classes. General and exam English, conversation practice etc.




See London, Learn English!

A guided walk of Central London for anyone learning English as a foreign language. See London's most famous sights. Hear about London's history in easy to understand English. Lots of time to chat and practise English too. Small groups. Other routes available. Visit my Airbnb Experience page to book or contact me directly.




About Me

I am an official City of Westminster guide and a qualified English teacher (CELTA/PTTLS/AET).

I am originally from London but have spent time living abroad so I also speak Spanish and French.

Over the last ten years I have enjoyed teaching English to students from around the world and more recently (pre-covid) showing international tourists my beautiful city.
